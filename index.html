The Start of Something New

First Article funsies
woo

I’ve seen blogs on Tumblr and personal vlogs on YouTube, always thinking it was for the entertainment of others. I never thought I would get into it.

Well, that has changed since you’re on my first page! As a sophomore in the University of Michigan, I heard of this inevitable plague called the sophomore slump. In summary, sophomore slump is when your mental, physical, spiritual, basically everything spirals down and you hit a wall. Hearing about this from upperclassmen and personally feeling it creep up, I sought an outlet to keep myself busy, take time to reflect on my life, and recognize everything that I’m investing me time in.

This is an experiment for me – a scary but exciting one. I am not one to document the events in my life whether it is academic or with friends. Writing, filming, taking pictures – I’m finding my own voice, so feel free to join the ride.
